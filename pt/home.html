ABOUT US

What we do

The  PQI – Portuguese Quantum Institute is a new institute devoted to research, innovation, education and outreach in Quantum Information Science and Technologies.

PQI builds on 18 years of experience in developing quantum information science and technology, including 8 European and 1 American research projects, and one doctoral programme.
