Welcome to DigiQ@Lisbon, the Portuguese cluster of project DigiQ – Digitally-Enhanced Quantum Technology Master 

 

DigiQ brings together 24 organisations from 10 European countries to provide training in Quantum Technologies, namely Quantum Computation, Quantum Networks, and Quantum Sensing and Metrology, as well as in complementary skills, such as Entrepreneurship in Quantum Technologies, Science Communication, etc.

 

DigiQ is not a master degree itself, it is an educational project providing master students from the universities participating in DigiQ with innovative online and in-person training opportunities, collaborative projects, mobility, and internships in industry and academia

 

The Portuguese cluster of project DigiQ is constituted by Instituto Superior Técnico, Deloitte Technology, and PQI – Portuguese Quantum Institute, all contributing to the project activities described above.

 

  • If you are / will be a master student at Instituto Superior Técnico in the academic year 2024/25, you can apply to join project DigiQ. Up to 15 students will be selected.

 

Students should take (or have taken) the course Introduction to Quantum Information and Computation.

 

An online meeting will provide selected students with more details about the project, as well as how ECTS credits could be obtained while participating in DigiQ activities throughout 2024/25.

To apply, fill in the application form by 04 September 2024.

Results will be communicated by 06 September 2024.

For enquiries, see our Frequently Asked Questions or use the contact information provided below.

 

  • If you are / will be a master student in one of the other universities participating in DigiQ in the academic year 2024/25, and you are interested in the opportunities offered by the DigiQ@Lisbon cluster, please contact us to the address below.
